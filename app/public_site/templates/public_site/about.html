WE ARE CONTRACTORZ

One Clear Workflow From Client Request To Payout.

Contractorz helps service businesses manage clients, capture job requirements, send quotes, coordinate work, invoice customers, and track payments in one place.

The field was moving, but the information was scattered.

A client request often begins in one place, the service details live somewhere else, and the quote, schedule, and invoice become separate records that the team must reconnect by hand.

Contractorz gives each client a service record that carries the address, questionnaire responses, quote, job history, and billing context through the workflow.

We built Contractorz so managers, employees, and clients can work from the same approved scope instead of rebuilding the story at every step.

The full service lifecycle in one place.

Every stage builds on the information already collected, from the first questionnaire through the final payout record.

Start With Complete Intake

Reusable service questionnaires collect the scope, address, and client details required before a quote is prepared.

Approve The Work Clearly

Businesses send numbered quotes with service-specific terms, and clients review and sign them through a secure link.

Connect Work And Billing

Jobs, invoices, Stripe payments, payouts, and refunds remain linked to the client service that created them.
